STEPS TO BOOK ROOM
To book a room, go to the book room page. This can be accessed by clicking on the book room link on the home page.
1. Select the hall of preference
2. Select the floor and room preferred. Make sure to confirm the booking and proceed to the payment page.
3. Payments can be made based on the provided options.
ROOM DETAILS
The room details help you know your roommates. This section has all details about your roommates, their program of study, and contact information.

You can also cancel your room over here.
WARNING
Payments are not refundable.

Rooms would be lost after the 48 hours of the grace period.

Rooms canceled would attract a fee of Gh50 on the next booking.
